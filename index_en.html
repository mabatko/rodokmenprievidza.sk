Find your ancestors in Slovakia – Discover your Slovak roots

For centuries Slovak immigration covered many countries. Searching for better social and work opportunities, adventure or hideout. Hundreds of thousands of men and women, often with children, moved into United States or Canada. Milions of Slovak descendants are now living in these countries. Some of them are now looking for their ancestors and their traces in history of Slovakia.

Send us your questions and we will search the records for you.

  • Creation of your Family tree, search for your ancestors and family relatives
  • Search for military records of your forefathers
  • Search for avalaible civil and church documents
  • History of your village and town
  • Assistance with your own search
